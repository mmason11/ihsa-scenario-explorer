Method: where actual IHSA assignments are unavailable, schools are grouped by recursive geographic bisection (balanced, contiguous splits), mirroring how sectional and regional boundaries are drawn; regional subdivisions are always modeled at the chosen size. Travel is one-way straight-line distance to the host site: the named IHSA sectional host where assignments are actual, otherwise the most centrally located member school (host schools travel 0). Any sectional's host can be reassigned from its roster panel — to another member, any IHSA school, or a custom venue by coordinates. School locations are city-level ZIP centroids, so figures understate true road miles by roughly 15–25% and Chicago-to-Chicago trips read as zero. Recommended private paths = 8 × private share of the field, rounded, kept between 1 and 4. Sources: 2025 girls flag football pairings; 2025–26 boys/girls lacrosse and boys volleyball assignments (Elgin and O'Fallon volleyball sectionals include sub-sectionals); 2024–25 water polo pairings. Charter and other non-boundaried public schools are treated as public. Verify status flags marked for review before publication.
